About UpStudent

Information about the services available to you

Priority One: You

Personalized, flexible, and affordable

Our goal is to give students access to fantastic opportunities in high-ranking universities and to set them on a path to a rewarding career.

But just as important to us is our commitment to making college—and the admissions process—easily affordable to parents. In addition to helping you get the most from financial aid and scholarships, we strive to give you exceptional, valuable service at the lowest possible cost.

While most admissions counselors charge thousands of dollars for a standardized program, UpStudent offers a highly personalized program at a low, flat hourly rate with no additional fees. We schedule only as many hours as a student really needs, and clients can easily add or remove appointments as best fits their schedules and needs.

We know that when it’s time to make college decisions, every dollar matters. UpStudent offers powerful expertise from college students with amazing success stories, all at a price you can afford.

Method

Guidance with tests, applications, and finance

We take a multi-faceted approach customized for each individual student. The lesson plan will vary between students depending on the type of student and how far along the student is in the process of getting into college.

Here are a few of the ways in which we help students optimize their college opportunities:

  • Exploring universities and choosing a list of fitting schools
  • Succeeding in the SAT, SAT II, and ACT tests
  • How to select helpful classes and extracurricular activities
  • How to productively spend summers in high school
  • How to create an outstanding application
  • Writing successful application essays and writing supplements
  • Selecting colleges and affording education
