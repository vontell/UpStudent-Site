Hours and Fees

Rate: $25/hr
Hours: Contact individual advisors

Tutors will establish a schedule with clients individually, in order to create a plan that works best for everyone. Weekends and evenings will likely be the optimal times for tutors, students, and parents.

Clients will be charged $25/hr flat rate, payed directly to their tutor. A client can schedule any number of hours per week with a tutor as long as the tutor is available for those hours. He is currently pursuing a bachelor’s degree in neurobiology from Harvard.

Logistics

Contact: Skype, Phone, Email, and Face-to-Face

Tutors and clients can arrange to meet in person in a suitable location, or to communicate via telephone or Skype.

A client can cancel an appointment and not be charged for the hour(s) as long as the tutor is notified at least 24 hours in advance.

A tutor should give at least 72 hours’ notice if he/she needs to cancel an appointment.

Parents are not required to be present during meetings, but they are encouraged to be present in order to be fully informed.

Method

Guidance with tests, applications, and finance

We take a multi-faceted approach customized for each individual student. The lesson plan will vary between students depending on the type of student and how far along the student is in the process of getting into college.

Here is a list of all the topics for which we can provide assistance to students. We will counsel students in all topics which are applicable for the individual:

  • Exploring universities and choosing a list of fitting schools
  • Succeeding in the SAT, SAT II, and ACT tests
  • How to select helpful classes and extracurricular activities
  • How to productively spend summers in high school
  • How to create an outstanding application
  • Writing successful application essays and writing supplements
  • Selecting colleges and affording education
