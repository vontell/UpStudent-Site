Our Plan for You

Our highly customized advisory program is designed to guide students and parents through every step of the college admissions journey. Our goal is to help students strengthen every facet of their applications, allowing them to approach college with complete confidence.

Stage One: Customized College Search

In this step we help students develop an optimal, customized list of safety, target, and reach schools based on the student’s unique personality, interests, learning style, and future goals. We also provide parents with surprising information about college affordability and the financial aid process. In addition, we discuss college researching as well as how to plan college visits and touring.

Stage Two: A Strong and Complete Application

In the main stage of coaching, tutors work one-on-one with students to build an outstanding, complete student profile and exceptional application to send to colleges. Our tutors were admitted to many of the most selective schools in the nation, and can share their first-hand experience with the application process. In our in-depth, comprehensive approach, we coach students in each of the following subjects:

  • How to choose the right courses and extracurricular activities while in high school.
  • How to productively and effectively spend summers in high school.
  • The importance of test scores and how to succeed on standardized tests.
  • How to maximize the impact of recommendation letters and whom to choose as your recommenders.
  • How to make interviews count. We’ll guide you through every step of the process and hold mock interviews so that you can walk in with confidence, make the most of the conversation, and walk out knowing you made a meaningful impression.
  • Every aspect of the CommonApp and unique applications. Here we help students compose essays of the highest quality and answer writing supplements in an outstanding and unique way. Our goal is to bring out a student’s best writing while presenting a complete and inimitable picture of the student that will have a powerful impact on admissions officers.

Stage Three: You’re in! Choosing the ideal college for you

We meticulously guide students in researching additional information on colleges and universities and weight compatibility parameters in order to ensure that each student is matched with the right institution once all letters of admission have been received.
